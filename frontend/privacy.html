Privacy Policy

We built Dov-tek on a simple principle: your messages are yours. We analyse them, we warn you, and then we discard them.

Effective: 1 May 2025
Updated: 1 May 2025
Version: 1.0
The short version — plain English
  • We never store the content of messages you scan
  • We store only scan metadata — risk level, score, timestamp
  • We collect your email when you create an account
  • We never sell your data to anyone
  • You can delete your account and all data at any time
  • We do not store message content — not temporarily, not anywhere
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
01

Who We Are

Dov-tek is a fraud detection service that analyses messages for signs of fraud and delivers verdicts to users. Operated by Dov-tek Technologies.

Contact: dovtekofficial@gmail.com

02

What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

Data TypeWhatWhyRetention
AccountEmail, hashed passwordTo create your accountUntil you delete
Scan metadataRisk level, score, timestampTo show scan historyUntil you request deletion

What we never collect: The content of any message you scan. It is analysed, a verdict is generated, and the message is permanently discarded.

03

Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, delete your account and all associated data, and export your scan history. Contact us at dovtekofficial@gmail.com for any request.

Questions about your privacy?

We respond to any data enquiry within 30 days.
